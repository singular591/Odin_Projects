Carbonara recipe

How to make Carbonara Pasta

Ingredients :

  • 6 ounces bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 pound orzo
  • 5 cups chicken stock (homemade or low-salt broth)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, minced
  • 3/4 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper
